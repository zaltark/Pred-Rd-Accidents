Deep Dive: The Effect of Temporal Features on Accident Risk

Holiday vs. Time of Day

This plot compares the mean accident risk throughout the day for holidays vs. non-holidays. We can look for differences in the daily pattern. For example, is the morning peak in accident risk (if any) different on holidays when there is no morning commute?

School Season vs. Time of Day

This plot compares the mean accident risk throughout the day for school season vs. non-school season. We can look for differences in the daily pattern, especially during morning and afternoon times, which correspond to school-related traffic.
